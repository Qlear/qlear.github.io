The Beginning

Qlear started as a development team of friends

Qlear started as a development team of friends. We spent countless hours trying to improve how our company delivered software to its customers asking one simple question: “Is this really the best we can do?”

We took all of our knowledge and research to identify the root cause of problems real employees face every day. Our goal was to solve the problem at its core and not just create a new process to fix a symptom. What we found was that the culture of the company didn’t support the changes we were suggesting.

During this work we discovered we were more passionate about the discussions we were having to improve the company, rather than the product we were creating. Once we realized this passion, there was no other option than to continue learning and improving. Qlear was born.

Our Process

We value conversations over dictations. That’s why we start with face to face conversations to discuss the current state of a company. We use all of this information to learn about the heart of the company; its culture. This allows us to identify what behaviors and actions the current culture supports and what it will take to lead a company in the direction they want to grow.

As our name implies, we strive to provide clear directions to improve the quality of any company starting at their core. Each of us have the discipline and passion to work through any issue in order to find the best solution. We ensure these solutions revolve around the people of a company, not the processes.
